MiaLoo – Support

Support page for the MiaLoo educational app for toddlers (ages 1–5).

Contact

If you have any questions, need help with the app, or want to report a problem, please contact me directly by email:

Email: assistance.MiaLoo@outlook.com

When you contact me, please include:

  • The name of the app: MiaLoo
  • The device model (for example: iPhone 14, iPad 9th generation)
  • The operating system version (for example: iOS 17)
  • A brief description of the issue

Parents and Privacy

MiaLoo is designed for very young children. The app does not collect personal data and does not contain ads.

For full details on how data and privacy are handled, please read the Privacy Policy .

You may also review the Terms of Service for additional information regarding the use of MiaLoo.

Purchases and Subscriptions

MiaLoo can be installed and explored for free. Full access to all categories and languages is unlocked through a monthly subscription.

There are no hidden fees. You can cancel the subscription at any time from your account settings. The subscription renews automatically unless it is canceled.

Feedback and Suggestions

If you have ideas for new cards, categories or languages that would help your child, I would be happy to hear from you. Your feedback helps improve MiaLoo for all families.
